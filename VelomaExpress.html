About VelomaExpress

At VelomaExpress, we don't just move people — we move purpose. Since our launch in 2015, we've redifined long-distance travel in Kenya through dependable service, expectional comfort, and human- centtered innovation.

From Nairobi to Kitale, Nakuru, Eldoret, Kisumu, and Malaba, VelomaExpress bridges major towns and communities with smooth, on-time routes daily — making us the go-to- choice for both commuters and businesses seeeking reliable mobility solutions.

Our story began with a single goal: to make journeys easier and put smiles on passenger's faces along the way.Whether you're riding for work, family, or leisure — or shipping a package — you can trust VelomaExpress to get you there, safely and comfortably.

We've earned our reputation as one of Kenya's fastest-growing transport providers by combining passion, purpose, and performance.
